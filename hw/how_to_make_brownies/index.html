How To Make The Best Brownies in The World in simple steps!

In this tutorial you will learn how to make the best brownies in simple steps.

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

  • Gather all of the necessary ingredients:

      Batter

    • 1/2 cup butter
    • 1 cup white sugar
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/3 cup sweetened cocoa powder
    • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

      • Frosting

    • 3 tablespoons butter
    • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1 tablespoon honey
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 cup confectionary sugar

  • In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

  • Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.

  • To Make Frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
